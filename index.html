What is Remote Client?

Remote Client is a standalone application for the already amazing Remote Messages. This app aims not only to retain Remote Message's awesomeness, by supporting each and every of its native features, but it also attempts to bring even more efficiency, comfort and convenience.
This started as a personal project that I've decided to share here for those who might be interested. If you have any constructive comment, suggestion or request, please check this link.



How to use it?

  • First, you'll need to have a jailbroken iDevice with the Remote Messages tweak installed and configured. For more information, see here.
  • Then, download and install Remote Client from this website (link at the top, or click here).
  • Launch the app and set you preferences up: you'll see every features you can enable/disable and everything you need to know should be written.
  • In the preferences window (press F1 to display it), click the "Tips and shortcuts" button at the bottom for more hidden features.
  • Start texting and... Enjoy!.

Features:

  • Automatic detection of the device’s IP address.

    If you’re not always on the same Wi-Fi, constantly checking and changing your IP can become laborious. The App does this for you: just save your device’s name and it finds your IP.

  • Text smileys replacement.

    Some of your contacts might not have an iPhone and send you text emoticons such as “:)”. Just like in BiteSMS, these are replaced by their corresponding Emoji.

  • Emoji shortcuts.

    Some of your contacts also have iPhone and you like emojis? Well place them easier and faster by defining custom text shortcuts that will be automatically replaced by the emojis you chose.

  • Notifications.

    Nobody likes to keep people waiting: when you get a message while the app is not visible, the icon will be flashing and a tooltip will appear.

  • Backgrounding and Notify Icon.

    When you close the app, it remains in background so that you can still be warned when you get a new message and go back to your messages instantly. An icon is displayed the notification area (at the right-bottom) allowing you to show the app or exit it.

  • Automatic Unfocus of a conversation.

    You might have let your browser opened on your computer and then forgot it so that some messages got read and you never answered it. The App automatically unfocuses a conversation after a certain delay (determined by you) so that this does not happen anymore.

  • Ghost Mode.

    Need some privacy? Chose a hotkey and activate the Ghost Mode: it allows you to quickly Hide the application with a simple press your hotkey so that it completely disappears. When you're alone again, press your hotkey again, enter your password and continue texting.

  • Fullscreen mode.

    No address bar, no title bar, just your messages.

  • Shortcuts.

    Typing on your computer rather than on your phone is more comfortable but also faster, uh? Well, be even more efficient with shortcuts to change conversation, send messages, etc.

  • and all Native features.

    Authentication, conversation list searching, viewing medias, etc.

Help:

  • Nothing is working?

    Remote Client's features are based upon Internet Explorer. If you have any trouble to get it working, check the version of Internet Explorer installed on your computer.
    Current version is 11.

  • Shortcuts.

    - Press F1 to display the preference window.
    - Press F2 to manually check for updates (automatically done at launch).
    - Press F5 to refresh the page (just in case of a problem).
    - Press F11 to switch to fullscreen mode.
    - Press F12 to automatically update your IP.
    - Press Alt+[1-9] to open the Nth conversation.
    - Press Ctrl+Enter instead of clicking the send button.
    - Press Ctrl+E to focus the sending area (editable text).

  • Specific features.

    Ghost Mode
    The Ghost Mode allows you to quickly and completely hide the application. In the preferences, you can chose your password and the hotkey that will toggle the Ghost mode.
    When hidden, you will not get any notification and the icon in the system tray will be invisible.
    To use the app again, press your hotkey and enter your password.
    If you have this feature disabled, you can still use the hotkey to Focus/Show/Hide the application.
    Emoji Shortcut
    You love the Emojis but using your mouse is really too much to bother?
    Well, don't worry: by right-clicking an icon in the emoji pane, you will be asked to define a word (or sign, or letter... Whatever you want!) that will be linked to the emoji you chose.
    Then, while texting, just press the little smiley-icon next to the 'Send' button, or press F10 to replace the text by the matching emojis.
